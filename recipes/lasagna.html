Lasagna

Description

This is one of the iconic dishes from Italy

Follow these easy steps to prepare the perfect lasagna

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Minced meat
  • Parmigiano
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Take a tray and spread olive oil
  2. Boil the pasta for 15 minutes
  3. Pour the pasta in the tray together with the mozzarella
  4. Spread Parmigiano on top
  5. Cook for 40 minutes
