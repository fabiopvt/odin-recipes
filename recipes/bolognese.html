Bolognese

Description

This is one of the iconic dishes from Bologna

Follow these easy steps to prepare the perfect bolognese

Ingredients

  • Tagliatelle
  • Minced meat
  • Parmigiano
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Take a pot and let water boil
  2. Add the pasta and cook for for 15 minutes
  3. Add tomato and minced meat
  4. Spread Parmigiano on top
